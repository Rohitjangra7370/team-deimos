Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective: May 2026

This website is operated by Team Deimos, a student team registered under the Dean of Student Affairs, IIT Mandi (H.P., India). We collect minimal data and use it only to respond to sponsorship enquiries.

What We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • Company or organisation name
  • Your message

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or behavioural advertising tools.

How We Use Your Data

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your sponsorship or partnership enquiry
  • Send you our sponsorship brochure or follow-up materials you request
  • Communicate about a potential or existing sponsorship agreement

We will not use your data for marketing unrelated to Team Deimos sponsorship, and we will not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party.

Data Storage & Processors

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Supabase — stores contact form submissions. Data is hosted on servers in the United States. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. (Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — delivers email notifications to our team when a form is submitted. Your message may pass through Resend's servers. (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — hosts this website. Vercel may log standard server access data (IP address, browser type, request time) for security and reliability purposes. (Privacy Policy)

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for analytics or advertising. Vercel and Supabase may set strictly necessary technical cookies required for the site to function. These cookies do not track you across websites.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. This will not affect your ability to read the site, but the contact form may not function correctly.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for a maximum of 24 months from the date of submission, or until the conclusion of any sponsorship relationship, whichever is later. Submissions with no further correspondence are deleted after 12 months.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to any legal obligation to retain it)
  • Withdraw consent to further communication at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Himachal Pradesh, India.
